Invisibility potion

Ingredients:

  • Phoenix blood
  • 100 Tea leaves
  • Cammomile
  • Perfectly black mouldy bannanas
  • Charcoal

Method:

  1. Pour the Phoenix blood into a bowl
  2. Powder the charcoal then add it with the phoenix blood
  3. Whisk it
  4. Dry the perfectly black mouldy bannanas in cammomile for 48 hours
  5. Put the bannanas in the blender for 1 hour
  6. Dry the tea leaves for a day
  7. Blend all of them together
  8. Boil them in a chamber pot for 48 hours with 89 celsius
  9. Leave it 3 weeks then it will become clear

    10. WARNING:Make sure to take 5 ml only
